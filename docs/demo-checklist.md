# Demo Checklist

Keep the video between 30 and 60 seconds.

1. Start the app.
2. Show `Living Breath` mode.
3. Change on-screen content so the sampled color mood changes.
4. Show the LED strip matching the screen mood.
5. Play audio and show gentle pulse response.
6. Click `Find LEDs`.
7. Click `Test LEDs`.
8. Open `http://ESP32_IP/status` in a browser.
9. Launch the portable `.exe`.
10. End on the LED strip breathing in sync with the app.
